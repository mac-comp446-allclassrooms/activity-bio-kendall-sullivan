Tal Rabin

Dr. Tal Rabin is a computer scientist known for her extensive work in cryptography.

Rabin studied at the University of Pennsylvania and the Thomas J. Watson Research Center.

One things that Rabin is currently working on is the Women In Theory Workshop, which she founded.

Rabin is on the 2018 Forbes Top 50 Women in Tech list. She is a fellow of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. She has also co-authored over 100 papers.
